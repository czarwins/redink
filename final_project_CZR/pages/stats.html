RED!NK was founded at the turn of the millenia in 1999 by two legendary tattoo artists, Skills McGee and Stick Hendricks. Skills comes from a long line of historic tattoo artists starting his journey in Decatur, GA. Skills opened his first tattoo studio back in 1995, where he changed the tattoo world by bringing what he had learned early on in his career traveling the country and tattooing next to many legendary artists. Stick got his start in Miami, Fl, where he not only learned the art form but he also learned the business side of the art. Stick was able to develop shrewd business strategies that grew RED!NK from humble beginnings to the top of their market. Together these iconic tattoo artists/entrepreneurs are paving the way for new paradigms in the world of body art.

At RED!NK, each tattoo artist perfects a unique style, which ensures that each of our clients are tattooed by experts in their respective styles. Our award winning staff prides itself on professionalism and providing a private studio atmosphere. We custom design all of our art and we work with our clients to capture the depths of their imagination in a visual manifestation that stays with them for a lifetime.
