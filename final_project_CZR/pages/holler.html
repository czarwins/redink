Skills McGee

Stick Hendricks

Co-founder of RED!NK Tatoo Artisans. Originally from Miami, FL, Stick has over 30 years of experience tattooing professionally. Stick got his start at Ocean View Tattoo & Piercing in Miami, FL. Stick specializes in realism and neo tradtional art.

Tommy Finch

Tommy is an all around bad ass with a tattoo gun. He's originally from Sacramento, CA and started tattooing at the age of 16. Tommy got his start at Red Moon Tattoo Parlor in Los Angeles, CA. Tommy Finch is an award winning artist that travels around the world to showcase his art. Tommy specializes in blackwork.

Smitty

A man that goes by one name is a man that means business. Originally from Philadelphia, PA, Smitty got his start on the east coast tattooing at various shops from New York City down to New Jersey. Smitty is what you call a competitive tattoo artist, flying around the globe to body art competitions. Smitty specializes in color work, tradtional tattooing, and new school.

Trinity James

Trinity James is our most creative artist. Ms. James began as a classically trained artist holding a Bachelor's degree in Viusal Art from The Juilliard School. Ms. James hails from Chicago, IL but got her start in New York City after completing her degree. Trinity James has been in the industry for over 10 years and is world-renowned. Trinity James specializes in illustration and piercing.

Mouse McGee

Mouse McGee, son of owner Skills McGee, has carved his own path in the tattoo world. Commonly referred to as a celebrity tattoo artist, Mouse has utilized social media to promote his art work and the RED!NK brand. Mouse has a clientele list that includes some of the biggest names in entertainment. Mouse specializes in tribal and blackwork.

Rachel Lovelace

Rachel Lovelace has been a gift from God! Rachel is the shop manager. She's the best tattoo shop manager in North America...if not the world. Rachel holds and MBA from the Robinson College of business at Georgia State university. Mrs. Lovelace has been an integral member of the RED!NK team for the last 5 years.
